Goldfish
Goldfish are commonly known as pets kept in indoor aquariums, and are one of the most popular aquarium fish.
Codfish
Cod is the common name for the demersal fish genus Gadus.
Lobster
Lobsters have long bodies with muscular tails, and live in crevices or burrows on the sea floor.
Salmon
Salmon are native to tributaries of the North Atlantic and Pacific Ocean.
Shark
Modern sharks are classified within the clade Selachimorpha and are the sister group to the rays.
